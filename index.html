Keeping Neocaridina Shrimp

Neocaridina shrimp (widely known as cherry shrimp) are small, colorful freshwater shrimp that have become one of the most popular ways to get started in the aquarium hobby. They are hardy, easy to care for, and come in bright colors like red, blue, yellow, and green. The Shrimp Sheet pulls together everything a new keeper needs all in one place, from setting up your first tank to raising a thriving colony.

Why Neocaridina?

These shrimp stay small, produce very little waste, and breed readily in a stable tank, which makes them a great first aquatic pet. Whether you want a low-maintenance pop of color or a full breeding project, Neocaridina are a beginner-friendly place to start.
